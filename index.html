About Me

Thank you for visiting my website! My name is Vu, I am currently pursuing Bachelor Degree in Computer Science at Wentworth Institute of Technology in Boston, Massachusetts.
I spent 2 years studying advanced Mathematics in Viet Nam before coming to Boston to finish high school. Realizing how much I enjoy sitting at my computer playing games and creating stuff, I decided to pursue my major and picked up Java for the first time at the age of 17. I never look back ever since.
I got hooked by the thrill of seeing my creations running on the monitor and my goal is to help others find a mean to realize their creativity the way I did. Hopefully, they would create something cool that may change the world.
